Bachelor's Degree vs Income Machine Learning

For our project we found the correlation between having a bachelor's degree and income. We collected data from the Bureau of Economic Analysis and the Nationa Center for Science and Engineering Statistics websites, both of our sources can be found below. With this data we began by cleaning with Pandas and then conducted Machine Learning and made some data visualizations with Tableau. The variables we tested include the percentage of bachelor degrees by state per year, the percentage of Post-Secondary degree by state per year, and the average income by state per year. Please click on either machine learning or tableau analysis below to see our results and observations.
